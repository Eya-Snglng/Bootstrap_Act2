We Are OohTako!

OohTako was born in 2014 from a deep love for authentic Japanese street food and a passion for creating the perfect takoyaki experience. What started as a small food stall quickly grew into a go-to spot for takoyaki lovers, known for its crispy-on-the-outside, creamy-on-the-inside perfection. With a commitment to high-quality ingredients, bold flavors, and a touch of innovation, OohTako continues to serve up irresistible takoyaki that keeps customers coming back for more!
